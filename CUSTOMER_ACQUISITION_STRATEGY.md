## Key Insights

**Austin-Specific Advantages:**
1. Tech-savvy population (early adopters)
2. Hot climate = critical AC maintenance (urgency)
3. Fast-growing city = new homeowners need help
4. Strong neighborhood community culture (Nextdoor works well)

**Best Channels for Orbzy:**
1. Nextdoor (hyperlocal + trusted)
2. Realtor partnerships (warm leads)
3. Facebook groups (engaged homeowners)
4. Google Ads (high intent)
5. Instagram (visual before/after content)

**Channels to Avoid:**
- Traditional radio (expensive, hard to track)
- Billboard ads (low conversion for app signups)
- TV commercials (budget too small for impact)
